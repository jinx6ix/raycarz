ENVIRONMENT VARIABLES REQUIRED
================================================================================

SMTP_PORT=587
SMTP_SECURE=false
SMTP_USER=your-email@gmail.com
SMTP_PASSWORD=your-16-digit-app-password
HOST_EMAIL=bookings@yourdomain.com
NEXT_PUBLIC_SITE_URL=https://yourdomain.com
